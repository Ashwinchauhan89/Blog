Here are some practical tips to help you stay focused:

1. Eliminate Distractions

Put your phone on silent or use tools like Focus Mode. Close unrelated tabs and apps.

2. Set Clear Goals

Before writing, define your objective. Are you writing a tutorial, a story, or an opinion piece?

3. Use a Timer

Try the Pomodoro Technique — write for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break.

4. Create a Writing Ritual

Light a candle, make a coffee, or play soft music. A ritual helps signal your brain it's time to write.

Focus isn't just about discipline — it’s also about environment, mindset, and clarity. Try a few of these techniques and make them part of your writing routine.
